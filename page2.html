Inspection

Abyss Solutions was engaged by Lamprell to complete a paint defect survey using aerial drone technology. Details of the inspections are as follows:

Date

Date: 26 April 2021
Time: 8:00am GST
Duration: 8 hrs

Equipment

Drone: Parrot ANAFI USA
Cameras: XT7 and M20

Operational

Temperature: 35 degrees celcius
Wind speed: < 10km/h
Visibility: Good

Scope

GVI: Full survey at 5m set back
CVI: Localised zoom survey at 2m set back of exterior of legs and diagonal members

Analysis

Data collected was subjected to Abyss Solutions' AI driven pipeline. Details of the analysis are as follows:

1

3D modeling & image localisation

Data collected from the survey is processed to produce a scale accurate 3D virtual replica of the structure. The position of every image is determined algorithmically using navigation data and visual features in the imagery.

2

Defect detection

Machine learning based algorithms trained on data provided by Lamprell were applied to automatically identify defects within the imagery. Unique instances of each defect were isolated.

3

Reporting

The location of each defect was overalid on the 3D model of the structure with defect imagery and information tied to this.
